## 🌐 Recommended Free Resources

These are universally useful and **completely free**:

### 📺 YouTube Channels
- **[BBC Learning English](https://www.youtube.com/@bbclearningenglish)** — World-class lessons for all levels.
- **[English Addict with Mr. Steve](https://www.youtube.com/@EnglishAddictwithMrSteve)** — Live English lessons from the UK.
- **[E2 IELTS](https://www.youtube.com/@E2IELTS)** — One of the best free IELTS prep channels.
- **[Rachel's English](https://www.youtube.com/@rachelsenglish)** — American pronunciation specialist.

### 📖 Reading & Listening
- **[Oxford Bookworms](https://elt.oup.com/catalogue/items/global/graded_readers/oxford_bookworms_library/)** — Graded readers for every level.
- **[Wikipedia (Simple English)](https://simple.wikipedia.org)** — Easy reading on any topic.
- **[News in Levels](https://www.newsinlevels.com/)** — Daily news in 3 difficulty levels.

### ✍️ Practice & Tools
- **[Quizlet](https://quizlet.com/)** — Flashcards for vocabulary.
- **[Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net/)** — Powerful spaced-repetition tool.
- **[Cambridge Dictionary](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/)** — Best free English dictionary.
- **[Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/)** — Free writing assistant.

### 🎯 IELTS-Specific
- **[Official IELTS Website](https://www.ielts.org/)** — Sample tests and official info.
- **[British Council IELTS Prep](https://takeielts.britishcouncil.org/take-ielts/prepare)** — Free preparation materials.
- **[IELTS Liz](https://ieltsliz.com/)** — Free tips, lessons, and model answers.

## 📅 Suggested Study Plan

| Stage | Duration | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| 🟢 **Foundation** | 1 – 2 months | Basic grammar, 1000 most common words |
| 🟡 **Skill Building** | 3 – 6 months | All four skills (Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking) |
| 🔵 **Deep Practice** | 6 – 12 months | Reading real content, watching shows, writing daily |
| 🔴 **Exam Prep** | 1 – 3 months | Official IELTS materials, mock tests, vocabulary mastery |

---

## 💡 Universal Tips for Every Learner

1. **Consistency beats intensity** — 30 minutes daily > 5 hours once a week.
2. **Practice all four skills** — Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.
3. **Read what you love** — Motivation is the fuel of learning.
4. **Don't translate everything** — Try to understand from context.
5. **Speak from day one** — Even to yourself or your mirror.
6. **Mistakes are progress** — Embrace them.
7. **Be patient** — Language learning is a journey, not a race.
